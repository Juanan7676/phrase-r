<aim>This article provides a comprehensive review of low emissions combustion technologies for modern aero gas turbines </aim>. <aim>The review considers current high Technologies Readiness Level (TRL) technologies including Rich-Burn Quick-quench Lean-burn (RQL), Double Annular Combustor (DAC), Twin Annular Premixing Swirler combustors (TAPS), Lean Direct Injection (LDI) </aim>. <aim>It further reviews some of the advanced technologies at lower TRL. <aim2> These include NASA multi-point LDI, Lean Premixed Prevaporised (LPP), Axially Staged Combustors (ASC) and Variable Geometry Combustors (VGC) </aim2>.
