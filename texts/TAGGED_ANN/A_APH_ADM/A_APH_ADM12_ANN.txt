<state>Two-dimensional metal oxides have been the focus of intense research in recent years, offering the opportunity for their use in energy conversion and storage devices, such as lithium-ion, sodium-ion, lithium–sulfur, and lithium–air batteries </state>. <state>In article number 1700176, Ziqi Sun and co-workers review recent progress in this field, along with the current challenges and opportunities for their application toward high energy density, high power density, and cyclic stability </state>
